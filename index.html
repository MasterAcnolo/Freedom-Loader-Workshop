1

Submission Requirements

  • Custom theme must follow the Freedom Loader format
  • Include a screenshot/preview of your theme (PNG, JPG - min 1200x800px)
  • Prepare your theme JSON with all metadata
  • Respect the color scheme and naming conventions
2

Submission Process

  1. Create a new GitHub issue on the repository
  2. Provide your theme's screenshots and list of modifications
  3. Attach or paste your theme JSON configuration
  4. Upload your preview image
3

Review & Integration

Once submitted, the maintainers will review your theme:

  • Format validation
  • Quality review of design
  • Once approved, your theme will be added to the Browse section

Freedom Loader Workshop is a powerful tool for creating and managing custom themes for Freedom Loader.
